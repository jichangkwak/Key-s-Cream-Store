Ice Cream Categories

Check out the diverse range of ice cream categories available in our store!. From grab-and-go sticks to satisfying family tubs, explore our packaged ice cream categories below

Cup Series

The Ice Cream Cup category consists of individually portioned frozen desserts packaged in small, disposable containers, typically made from plastic or heavy paperboard. These are designed for quick, single-serving consumption and are often sold complete with a miniature spoon, making them highly convenient for a ready-to-eat treat..

Cup

Stick Series

The Ice Cream Stick (also known as an Ice Cream Bar) features a molded piece of ice cream served on a handle, usually a wooden or plastic stick. This format is a popular "grab-and-go" dessert, perfect for eating outdoors or on the move. Sticks are commonly enrobed in a layer of chocolate, nuts, or a flavored shell, offering a contrasting texture to the smooth ice cream inside.

Stick

Cone Series

The Ice Cream Cone category refers to ice cream pre-packaged inside an edible, crunchy wafer or sugar cone. This combines the creamy smoothness of the ice cream with the enjoyable, crisp texture of the cone itself. Pre-packaged cones often feature decorative toppings and sometimes include a solid chocolate plug at the very bottom of the cone.

Cone

The 5 liter tub key's cream

The 5 Liter Tub is the largest commercial size for ice cream packaging. This bulk format is the most cost-effective option for high-volume users, including catering services, cafes, restaurants, or very large households. It is designed purely for scooping and offers a significant quantity of ice cream in one container.
